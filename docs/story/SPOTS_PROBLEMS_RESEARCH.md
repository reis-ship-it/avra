## üéØ **Primary Problems (Currently in SPOTS Story)**

### **1. Loneliness & Social Isolation**

**Statistics:**
- **40.3%** of American adults report feeling lonely at least occasionally
- **57%** of Americans report feeling lonely (up from 46% in 2018)
- **37.4%** experience moderate to severe loneliness (23.5% moderate, 14.0% severe)
- **60%** of young adults aged 18-24 report frequent feelings of isolation
- **49%** of Americans feel lonelier in 2025 than a decade ago
- U.S. Surgeon General declared loneliness a public health epidemic

**Health Impacts:**
- **29%** increased risk of heart disease
- **32%** increased risk of stroke
- **50%** increased risk of developing dementia in older adults
- Increased risks of depression, anxiety, high blood pressure, cognitive decline, obesity, weakened immune function
- Premature death risk equivalent to smoking 15 cigarettes per day

**How SPOTS Addresses:**
- AI-powered matching connects people with places and communities they'll genuinely connect with
- Event hosting creates real-world communities
- Expertise system builds authentic relationships through shared interests
- Third place discovery helps people find community spaces
- Real-world reconnection through technology
