## ‚ùì **Frequently Asked Questions**

### **Q: What makes SPOTS different from other location/event apps?**
**A:** Five things: (1) AI2AI system that learns your vibe through anonymous agent data, (2) Effortless discovery‚Äîno more decision fatigue, (3) Expertise system that unlocks economic opportunities, (4) True privacy‚Äîyour personal data never leaves your device, (5) Uses technology to help you disconnect from technology‚Äîfind real places, real people, real experiences.

### **Q: How does the AI2AI system work?**
**A:** Think of it like two people having a conversation without sharing personal details. Your phone learns what you like (on your device, privately), then talks to other phones to find matches‚Äîbut never shares who you are. It's like a matchmaker who knows your preferences but never tells anyone your name.

**Technical:** Each user has an AI agent that learns on-device. These agents exchange anonymous "vibe signatures" with each other‚Äîno personal data is shared. The agents learn from successful connections to improve recommendations, all while keeping your personal information completely private.

### **Q: How do users make money?**
**A:** Making money is optional. SPOTS is free for users‚Äîthey find community, know what's good around them, and use group matching. Some users become local experts in their passions (coffee, food, events, etc.). Once they reach Local expertise level, they can host events. They can partner with businesses, charge for events, and build a following. Many users turn this into full-time income, but it's not required‚Äîmany users just enjoy finding community and knowing what's good around them.

### **Q: How do you protect user privacy?**
**A:** We only analyze agent data‚Äîcompletely anonymous from users. Your personal information never leaves your device. We can share valuable insights with businesses (like IBM, McKinsey, Google) to make the world better, while you remain completely anonymous.

### **Q: What's the business model?**
**A:** 
- **For Users:** Completely free - never pay SPOTS anything. Discover places, connect with communities, attend events - all free.
- **For Experts/Businesses:** Free to become expert or partner, 10% platform fee on revenue you make from events or deals (you keep 90%)
- **For Enterprises:** Custom pricing for white-label solutions
- **Future Revenue:** Privacy-preserving data insights for businesses and enterprises

### **Q: What's the market opportunity?**
**A:** Combines location discovery ($50B+), event management ($5B+), and business intelligence markets. Plus addresses a public health crisis affecting over half of Americans.

### **Q: What's your competitive advantage?**
**A:** 30 patent innovations, privacy-preserving AI2AI technology, expertise-based economic model, and offline-first architecture. No one else has this combination.

### **Q: How far along is SPOTS?**
**A:** 185,000+ lines of code, Phase 7 of 20 complete (83%), 6 phases fully done. 30 patents filed. Production-ready platform. Beta launch planned for Q1 2026, limited to first 1,000 users. Early traction: waitlist growing, 3 universities in pilot discussions, business partnerships in early conversations.

### **Q: What's your 5-year vision?**
**A:** 1M+ daily active users, corporate partnerships with universities and enterprises, a world where people find community, know what's good around them, and (optionally) can make a living sharing their passions. Work gets shared to the right people every single time.

### **Q: Why should I trust SPOTS with my data?**
**A:** We don't have your data. We only use anonymous agent data that can't be traced back to you. Your personal information stays on your device. It's the only platform that can share insights while protecting privacy completely.
