## üß† **How the Key Works**

### **1. Always Learning With You**

The AI learns **alongside** you:
- Which doors resonate with you
- When you're ready for new doors
- How you prefer to open doors
- What's on the other side of doors you've opened

**Not learning about you. Learning with you.**

---

### **2. Three Learning Modes**

#### **Personal Learning (On-Device)**
The key learns which doors YOU open:
- Which spots you visit
- How long you stay
- What you return to
- When you're receptive

#### **AI2AI Learning (Peer-to-Peer)**
The key discovers people who open similar doors:
- Bluetooth connections
- Personality exchange
- Compatibility matching
- People who might open doors together

#### **Cloud Learning (Optional Enhancement)**
The key gains wisdom from the network:
- Which doors lead where
- Popular vs. hidden doors
- Collective intelligence
- Network patterns

## üé≠ **Your Doors Stay Yours**

### **The Problem**
Without protection, everyone opens the same doors. You lose what makes you unique.

### **The Solution: Contextual Personality**

**Core Personality (Stable)**
- The doors that define YOU
- Maximum 30% drift from baseline
- Your authentic doors stay yours

**Contextual Layers (Flexible)**
- Work doors vs. social doors vs. exploration doors
- You open different doors in different contexts
- Adaptation without losing core identity

**Evolution Timeline (Preserved)**
- All doors you've ever opened
- Life phases preserved forever
- You can grow and change
- Your door history is never lost

**Result:** You can authentically change over time, but your doors stay yours. People can find you based on doors you're opening NOW or doors you opened THEN.
