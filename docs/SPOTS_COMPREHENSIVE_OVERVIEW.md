## üéØ **Executive Summary**

**SPOTS** is an offline-first, AI-powered mobile application that helps people discover meaningful places, build authentic communities, and find belonging through personalized recommendations and event hosting.

### **The Core Value Proposition**

SPOTS transforms how people discover places and connect with communities. Unlike traditional recommendation apps that push generic suggestions, SPOTS learns **with** users to open doors to:
- **Meaningful places** (coffee shops, bookstores, restaurants)
- **Authentic communities** (writing groups, food clubs, local meetups)
- **Life-changing experiences** (events, workshops, curated walks)
- **Belonging** (finding your people, your third place, your life)

### **Key Differentiators**

1. **"Doors, Not Badges"** - Authentic contributions unlock features, not gamification
2. **AI2AI Learning** - Privacy-preserving peer-to-peer AI learning between devices
3. **Offline-First** - Works without internet, syncs when connected
4. **No Pay-to-Play** - Recommendations never for sale, authentic discovery
5. **Expertise Recognition** - Users become experts through genuine contributions

### **Business Model**

- **10% platform fee** on all paid events and transactions
- **Event ticketing** (hosts, partnerships, sponsorships)
- **Business partnerships** (venues, brands, organizations)
- **Future: AI learning data** (anonymized, aggregate insights)

### **Technical Foundation**

- **Flutter/Dart** - Cross-platform mobile app (iOS/Android)
- **185,162+ lines of code** across 549 files
- **Offline-first architecture** - Sembast local database
- **AI-powered personalization** - On-device + AI2AI learning
- **Scalable backend** - Supabase, Firebase, Stripe

## üö™ **Philosophy & Mission**

### **Core Philosophy: "Doors, Not Badges"**

SPOTS recognizes that authentic contributions matter more than gamification:

- ‚úÖ **Doors** - Features that open access to experiences and communities
- ‚ùå **Badges** - Empty achievements that don't provide real value

**What This Means:**
- Expertise unlocks **event hosting** (a real door)
- Lists unlock **community recognition** (a real door)
- Feedback unlocks **better recommendations** (a real door)
- **No meaningless badges, points, or leaderboards**

### **Core Values**

#### **1. Belonging Comes First**
SPOTS exists to help people find places, experiences, and communities where they truly feel at home‚Äîwherever they are. Every spot is a potential door to belonging.

#### **2. No Agenda. No Politics. No Pay-to-Play.**
SPOTS is a neutral, open platform. We don't push trends, politics, or whoever pays the most. **Our suggestions are never for sale.**

#### **3. Authenticity Over Algorithms**
Suggestions are powered by real data‚Äîpreferences, time spent, feedback‚Äînot advertising dollars or hidden motives. We help you discover what you'll actually enjoy.

#### **4. Privacy and Control Are Non-Negotiable**
Your data is yours. You control your preferences, your vibe, and your experience. We use data wisely, securely, and only to make your experience better.

#### **5. Community, Not Just Places**
Every spot is a door to community. Your favorite coffee shop isn't just a recommendation; it's a door to the writers' group. That trivia bar isn't just a spot; it's where you meet your crew.

#### **6. Always Learning With You**
The AI learns **alongside** you‚Äîwhich doors resonate, when you're ready for new doors, how you prefer to open doors. Not learning about you. Learning **with** you.
