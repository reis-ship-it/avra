## üí° **Tips for Efficient Searching**

1. **Start Broad, Then Narrow:**
   - First search: `assignee:IBM quantum`
   - Then narrow: `assignee:IBM quantum algorithm optimization`

2. **Use Citation Analysis:**
   - When you find a relevant patent, check its citations
   - Check patents that cite it (forward citations)

3. **Save Relevant Patents:**
   - Google Patents allows saving to collections
   - Create collections: "Patent #1 - Quantum", "Patent #3 - Personality", etc.

4. **Document as You Go:**
   - Don't wait until the end
   - Fill in `PRIOR_ART_SEARCH_RESULTS.md` as you find patents

5. **Focus on Relevance:**
   - Not every patent needs to be documented
   - Focus on patents that are most similar to yours
   - Document 5-10 most relevant per category
