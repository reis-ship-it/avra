## üéØ **Search Strategy**

### **Step 1: Broad Searches First**
- Start with broad queries to get overview
- Review first 20-30 results
- Identify most relevant patents

### **Step 2: Narrow Searches**
- Use specific assignees (IBM, Google, etc.)
- Use specific keywords from relevant patents
- Review citations in relevant patents

### **Step 3: Citation Analysis**
- For each relevant patent found:
  - Review backward citations (patents it cites)
  - Review forward citations (patents that cite it)
  - This often reveals more relevant prior art

### **Step 4: Document Findings**
- Fill in `PRIOR_ART_SEARCH_RESULTS.md` with findings
- Update patent documents with specific patent numbers
- Complete difference analysis
