## üìÖ Filing Timeline Options

### **Option 1: Sequential Filing (Recommended)**

**Timeline:** File one patent per month over 5 months

**Advantages:**
- Spreads costs over time
- Allows learning from first filing
- Reduces risk of simultaneous rejections
- Easier to manage

**Schedule:**
- Month 1: Patent #1
- Month 2: Patent #3
- Month 3: Patent #11
- Month 4: Patent #21
- Month 5: Patent #29

---

### **Option 2: Batch Filing (Fast Track)**

**Timeline:** File all 5 patents within 1-2 months

**Advantages:**
- Faster protection
- Earlier priority dates
- Potential attorney discounts

**Disadvantages:**
- Higher upfront costs
- More complex to manage
- Higher risk if issues arise

---

### **Option 3: Phased Filing (Balanced)**

**Timeline:** File in 2 phases

**Phase 1 (Months 1-2):** Core Patents
- Patent #1: Quantum Compatibility
- Patent #3: Contextual Personality Drift Resistance

**Phase 2 (Months 4-5):** Supporting Patents
- Patent #11: AI2AI Network Monitoring
- Patent #21: Offline Quantum Privacy
- Patent #29: Multi-Entity Entanglement

**Advantages:**
- Balances speed and cost
- Prioritizes core patents
- Allows adjustment based on Phase 1 results
