## üîî Important Dates

### **USPTO Response Deadlines:**
- **Office Actions:** 3 months (extendable to 6 months with fees)
- **Final Office Actions:** 2 months (extendable to 5 months)
- **Maintenance Fees:**
  - 3.5 years: Due at 3.5 years from grant
  - 7.5 years: Due at 7.5 years from grant
  - 11.5 years: Due at 11.5 years from grant

### **Priority Dates:**
- **Filing Date:** Establishes priority
- **Provisional Priority:** 12 months to file non-provisional
- **PCT Priority:** 12 months to file PCT, 18 months to enter national phase
