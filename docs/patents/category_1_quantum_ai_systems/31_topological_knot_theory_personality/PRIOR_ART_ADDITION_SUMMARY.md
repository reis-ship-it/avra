#### Knot Theory (+4 papers)

3. **Freyd, P., Yetter, D., Hoste, J., Lickorish, W. B. R., Millett, K., & Ocneanu, A. (1985)** - "A new polynomial invariant of knots and links"
   - HOMFLY polynomial definition
   - Additional polynomial invariant for knot classification

4. **Khovanov, M. (2000)** - "A categorification of the Jones polynomial"
   - Khovanov homology, categorification of knot invariants
   - Advanced knot invariant methods

5. **Morton, H. R., & Short, H. B. (1990)** - "The 2-variable polynomial of cable knots"
   - Cable knots, polynomial invariants
   - Knot operations and invariants

#### Topology (+2 papers)

6. **Otter, N., Porter, M. A., Tillmann, U., Grindrod, P., & Harrington, H. A. (2017)** - "A roadmap for the computation of persistent homology"
   - Persistent homology computation methods
   - Computational methods for topological analysis

7. **Chazal, F., & Michel, B. (2021)** - "An introduction to Topological Data Analysis: fundamental and practical aspects for data scientists"
   - Topological data analysis introduction
   - Demonstrates topological methods for data analysis, but not knot theory

#### Personality Psychology (+2 papers)

8. **DeYoung, C. G., et al. (2010)** - "Testing predictions from personality neuroscience: Brain structure and the big five"
   - Big Five neuroscience validation
   - Demonstrates biological basis for multi-dimensional personality models

9. **Roberts, B. W., & DelVecchio, W. F. (2000)** - "The rank-order consistency of personality traits from childhood to old age"
   - Personality stability over time
   - Foundation for understanding personality evolution and temporal tracking

#### Quantum Mechanics (+2 papers)

10. **Uhlmann, A. (1976)** - "The 'transition probability' in the state space of a *-algebra"
    - Bures distance and transition probability
    - Quantum distance metrics for compatibility calculations

11. **Jozsa, R. (1994)** - "Fidelity for mixed quantum states"
    - Quantum fidelity metrics
    - Quantum state similarity metrics for compatibility
