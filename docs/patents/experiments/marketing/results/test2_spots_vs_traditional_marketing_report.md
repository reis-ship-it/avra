# SPOTS vs Traditional Marketing Experiment Report

**Date:** December 21, 2025
**Experiment Duration:** 6 months
**Events per Group:** 500
**Users per Group:** 1000

### Marketing Timeline

**Traditional Marketing:**
- Marketing Start: 30-60 days before event
- Marketing Duration: 4-8 weeks (28-56 days)
- Longer lead time needed for broad reach campaigns

**SPOTS AI Marketing:**
- Marketing Start: 7-14 days before event
- Marketing Duration: 1-2 weeks (7-14 days)
- Shorter lead time due to faster, targeted matching

---

## Executive Summary

### Key Findings

- **Attendance Rate:** SPOTS 23.12% vs Traditional 10.47% (2.21x improvement)
- **Conversion Rate:** SPOTS 89.39% vs Traditional 0.10% (856.24x improvement)
- **Gross Revenue per Event:** SPOTS $11,558.82 vs Traditional $5,233.33 (2.21x improvement)
- **Cost per Acquisition:** SPOTS $5.20 vs Traditional $23.87 (4.59x better)
- **ROI:** SPOTS 3.37:1 vs Traditional 1.05:1 (3.21x improvement)
