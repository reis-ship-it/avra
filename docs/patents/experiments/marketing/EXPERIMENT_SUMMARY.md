## üéØ Experiment Overview

This experiment compares SPOTS AI-powered marketing systems against traditional event marketing techniques through two comprehensive tests:

### Test 1: Equal Time Comparison (Fair ROI)
- **Purpose:** Compare ROI with equal time investment
- **Setup:** Both groups use same marketing timeline (30-60 days before, 4-8 weeks duration)
- **Events:** 500 events per group
- **Users:** 1,000 users per group
- **Duration:** 6 months

### Test 2: Efficiency Advantage (More Events)
- **Purpose:** Show SPOTS can market more events due to shorter lead time
- **Setup:** Traditional limited by 30-60 day lead time, SPOTS uses 7-14 day lead time
- **Events:** Traditional: 6 events (1/month), SPOTS: 17 events (2.83x more)
- **Users:** 1,000 users per group
- **Duration:** 6 months
